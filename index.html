Made to melt

Inspired by simple fondue, the mug pairs ceramic shaped for candle heat with a space for chocolate. No fuss: light the candle, wait for it to melt, and serve. Brown, beige, and the white of chocolate shape the brand — warm, straightforward, and indulgent.

How it works

  1. 1

    Candle underneath

    Place the candle in the holder below the mug. Heat rises evenly.

  2. 2

    Chocolate melts

    The chocolate turns liquid — perfect for fruit, biscuits, or whatever you prefer.

  3. 3

    Serve and repeat

    Adjust the candle to hold the temperature. A sweet moment for you or to share.
